# CreditCardStatementPayload

Payload for a credit card monthly statement. Aligned with UK Open Banking OBStatement2 and FCA consumer credit disclosure requirements.

## Fields

| Field | Type | Required | Default | Description |
|-------|------|----------|---------|-------------|
| `statementId` | `string` | Yes | — | Unique identifier for this statement. |
| `accountId` | `string` | Yes | — | Reference to the credit card Account. |
| `maskedCardNumber` | `string` | Yes | — | Masked card number for display, e.g. '**** **** **** 1234'. |
| `accountHolderName` | `string` | Yes | — | Name of the primary cardholder. |
| `productName` | `string` | Yes | — | Credit card product name, e.g. 'Premier Rewards Credit Card'. |
| `statementDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 date the statement was generated. |
| `periodStartDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 start date of the billing period. |
| `periodEndDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 end date of the billing period. |
| `paymentDueDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 date by which payment must be received to avoid late fees. |
| `minimumPaymentAmount` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Minimum payment amount required by the due date. |
| `minimumPaymentDueDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 date by which the minimum payment must be received. |
| `statementBalance` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total balance owed as at the statement date. |
| `previousBalance` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Balance carried forward from the previous statement. |
| `paymentsReceived` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total payments received during the billing period. |
| `purchasesTotal` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total value of purchases in the billing period. |
| `cashAdvancesTotal` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Total cash advance amounts in the billing period. |
| `balanceTransfersTotal` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Total balance transfers in the billing period. |
| `interestCharged` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total interest charges applied in the billing period. |
| `purchaseInterestRate` | `string` | Yes | — | Annual purchase interest rate as a decimal string, e.g. '21.9'. |
| `cashAdvanceInterestRate` | `string` _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Annual cash advance interest rate as a decimal string. |
| `feesCharged` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Total fees charged in the billing period, e.g. late payment fees. |
| `creditLimit` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Current credit limit. |
| `availableCredit` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Available credit remaining at statement date. |
| `rewardsBalance` | `RewardsBalance` _(inline record)_ _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Rewards or cashback programme balance. |
| `transactions` | array of `com.bank.schemas.payloads.statements.StatementTransaction` | No | `[]` | Transactions in this billing period. |
| `correspondenceAddress` | [`Address`](./common/Address) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Address for printed statement delivery. |
