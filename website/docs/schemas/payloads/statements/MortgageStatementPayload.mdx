# MortgageStatementPayload

| | |
|---|---|
| **Namespace** | `com.bank.schemas.payloads.statements` |
| **Type** | `record` |
| **Version** | `1.0.0` |
| **Compatibility** | `BACKWARD` |
| **Status** | `ACTIVE` |
| **Source** | [schemas/payloads/statements/MortgageStatementPayload.avsc](https://github.com/samlarsen1/comms-docs-schemas/blob/main/schemas/payloads/statements/MortgageStatementPayload.avsc) |

Payload for a residential or buy-to-let mortgage annual or periodic statement. Aligned with FCA MCOB (Mortgage Conduct of Business) disclosure requirements and UK Finance mortgage statement standards.

## Fields

| Field | Type | Required | Default | Description |
|-------|------|----------|---------|-------------|
| `statementId` | `string` | Yes | — | Unique identifier for this statement. |
| `accountId` | `string` | Yes | — | Reference to the mortgage Account. |
| `mortgageReference` | `string` | Yes | — | Bank's mortgage reference number. |
| `accountHolderNames` | array of `string` | Yes | — | Names of all mortgage holders (primary and joint). |
| `productName` | `string` | Yes | — | Mortgage product name, e.g. '2-Year Fixed Rate Mortgage'. |
| `mortgageType` | `MortgageType` _(inline enum)_ | Yes | — | Repayment structure of this mortgage. |
| `propertyAddress` | [`Address`](./common/Address) | Yes | — | Address of the mortgaged property. |
| `statementDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 date the statement was generated. |
| `periodStartDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 start date of the statement period. |
| `periodEndDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 end date of the statement period. |
| `originalMortgageAmount` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Original mortgage advance amount. |
| `outstandingBalance` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Outstanding mortgage balance at end of statement period. |
| `currentInterestRatePercent` | `string` | Yes | — | Current annual interest rate as a decimal string. |
| `interestRateType` | [`InterestRateType`](./common/enums/InterestRateType) | Yes | — | Structure of the current interest rate. |
| `rateReviewDate` | `string` _(optional)_ | No | `null` | ISO 8601 date when the current rate period ends and the rate may change. |
| `svr` | `string` _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Standard Variable Rate the mortgage will revert to after the deal period, as a decimal string. |
| `monthlyPaymentAmount` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Current monthly payment amount. |
| `nextPaymentDate` | `string` | Yes | — | ISO 8601 date of the next scheduled payment. |
| `remainingTermMonths` | `int` | Yes | — | Number of months remaining on the mortgage term. |
| `totalPaidThisPeriod` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total amount paid during the statement period. |
| `capitalRepaidThisPeriod` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Capital portion of payments made during the period. |
| `interestChargedThisPeriod` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | Total interest charged during the statement period. |
| `feesChargedThisPeriod` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Total fees charged during the period. |
| `overpaymentAllowanceRemaining` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Remaining overpayment allowance for the current year without incurring early repayment charges. |
| `overpaymentsMadeThisPeriod` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Total overpayments made during the period. |
| `earlyRepaymentCharge` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Early repayment charge that would apply if the mortgage were redeemed today. |
| `estimatedRedemptionFigure` | [`Money`](./common/Money) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Estimated total amount required to fully redeem the mortgage today. |
| `transactions` | array of `com.bank.schemas.payloads.statements.StatementTransaction` | No | `[]` | Payment transactions during the statement period. |
| `correspondenceAddress` | [`Address`](./common/Address) _(optional)_ | No | `null` | Address for printed statement delivery. |
