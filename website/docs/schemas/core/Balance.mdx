Account balance at a point in time. Aligned with UK Open Banking OBReadBalance1 and ISO 20022 CashBalance8.

## Fields

| Field | Type | Required | Default | Description |
|-------|------|----------|---------|-------------|
| `balanceId` | `string` | Yes | — | Unique identifier for this balance record. |
| `accountId` | `string` | Yes | — | Reference to the Account this balance belongs to. |
| `balanceType` | [`BalanceType`](./common/enums/BalanceType) | Yes | — | The type of balance being reported. |
| `amount` | [`Money`](./common/Money) | Yes | — | The balance amount. |
| `creditDebitIndicator` | [`CreditDebitIndicator`](./common/enums/CreditDebitIndicator) | Yes | — | Whether the balance is a credit (in-credit) or debit (overdrawn) position. |
| `dateTime` | `long` _(timestamp-millis, UTC ms)_ | Yes | — | UTC timestamp at which this balance was calculated or reported. |
| `creditLine` | array of `CreditLine` _(inline record)_ | No | `[]` | Associated credit lines or overdraft facilities. |

## Nested Types
